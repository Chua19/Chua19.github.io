Avant Navigation Group Inc.

Technology-Propelled Responsible Perpetual Growth

Mission

The mission of ANG is to promote global inclusive insurance and inclusive finance by providing safety net and financial resources to emerging sustainable growth potentials.

Vision

Avant Navigation Group will become the successor to Berkshire Hathaway in Web3 era.

Definition

ANG is a licensed financial technology company headquartered in New York. It is committed to promoting Web3 FinAppChain architecture with a cloud-native digital insurance + digital bank + digital securities platform. The innovative structure is used to connect the ecosystems of digital finance islands to form a prosperous global Web3 inclusive financial ecosphere.

Unsatisfied Financial Needs in Developed Countries

ANG plans to use mobile Super App with seamless integration of efficient payment, credit, insurance, wealth management and other financial services. The user community is reshaped in the Web3 world with unique value creation mechanism.

Severely Inadequate Financial Supply in Developing Countries

ANG plans to integrate financial supply capabilities with a financial holding company designation. We will stimulate the capabilities of financial institutions through Web3 ecosystem and use Fintech capabilities to effectively meet inclusive financial needs
