Beginning 2000 BC: Insurance 1.0

Ancient Babylonian merchants first established commodity credit insurance

Renaissance, Age of Discovery: Insurance 2.0

In the Genoa region of Italy in the 14th century, a real insurance contract appeared

1666 Great Fire of London: Insurance 3.0

Insurance contracts have entered a period of standarization and have been brought into regulation through regulations

Actuarial science, which calculates the probability of loss, becomes the supporting technology of the modern insurance industry
